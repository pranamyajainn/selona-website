# Selona Design System

## Product Position

Selona is the company. ThinkAIWork is the flagship product: an AI-agnostic workspace and control layer for finance teams and fractional CFOs.

The site should communicate one idea clearly:

Controlled inputs in. Structured, auditable outputs out.

## Visual Direction

- Calm finance SaaS, not an automation agency.
- White and soft blue-grey surfaces with deep navy text.
- Selona blue is an action accent, used sparingly.
- Hairline borders and restrained shadows are preferred over glow.
- Cards use an 8px radius.
- No fake client logos, certifications, security claims, or fundraising language.

## Type

- Display: Satoshi 700.
- Body: Inter 400/500.
- Letter spacing stays at 0.
- Type sizes step by breakpoint instead of tracking viewport width.
- Headlines should be short and concrete.

## Motion

- Preserve the existing Selona hero loop videos in `public/brand/`.
- The hero word reveal stays subtle and respects `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- Section reveals should be brief fade-up transitions only.
- Ticker or marquee patterns should not be used unless real content requires them.

## Homepage Order

1. Hero
2. Problem
3. Controlled inputs to structured outputs
4. Six finance-owned layers
5. Use cases
6. Trust and auditability
7. Private beta proof
8. CTA
9. FAQs
10. Footer

## Copy Rules

- Write for finance buyers, not fundraising audiences.
- Avoid generic AI buzzwords.
- Use `ThinkAIWork by Selona` when introducing the product.
- Keep the public proof buyer-safe: private beta with finance operators, 10 fractional CFOs, and 3 partner channels only.
- Security wording should stay general unless formal documentation exists. Use “Security documentation available on request.”
